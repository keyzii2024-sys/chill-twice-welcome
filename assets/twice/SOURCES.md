## Website Design Notes

- Structure: full-screen video hero, role index, cinema / works / visual posters, interaction lab, first-view QA.
- PC layout: max content width around 1700px, large editorial rhythm, 8px radius, low-shadow glass.
- Mobile layout: one-column sections, compressed nav, optimized WebP assets, lightweight MP4 hero.
- Palette: goose yellow, white, airy blue, restrained deep-blue text, translucent glass material.
